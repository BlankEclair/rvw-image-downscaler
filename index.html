Rainverse Wiki Image Downscaler

This is a tool for the Rainverse Wiki to downscale images according to Wikipedia's requirements.

Created by BlankEclair and open sourced under the MIT license on Average Codestuff and Github.

Note for Firefox users with privacy.resistFingerprinting enabled: This site uses the Canvas APIs, which means that downloaded images will be garbled by default. to activate the permission popup.
